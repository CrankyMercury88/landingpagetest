            // Company Overview
          

          

            Vera.ML is revolutionizing how development teams understand their
            code changes and impact on business outcomes. We operate at the
            repository level, collecting and analyzing data directly from your
            codebase.
          

          

            // Our Approach
          

          

            Our platform provides
            real-time insights and
            actionable metrics that
            help teams make better decisions about their development process. By
            analyzing code patterns, commit histories, and integration with
            business data, Vera.ML creates a comprehensive picture of your
            development ecosystem that translates technical work into business
            value.
